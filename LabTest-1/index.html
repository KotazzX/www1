About Sault College

Sault College is a place to find the real you. Their main goal is to make sure you get the best quality of life in college and in the work field. Finally they have been the number one college in the province for about 9 years in a row.

A quote from Sault College's website:

We offer quality programming, qualified faculty, and an ambitious vision, and we are an organization filled with creative, hard-working, and dedicated staff and students learning in a space inspired by the beauty and opportunity of our Northern Ontario location.

Sault College is located in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario
